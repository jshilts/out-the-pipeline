# Careers, And Those Words (September 2012)

## 1. SUMMARY

Among those who stay, two broad pathways emerge:

* **Managerial track** – promotion brings more office work, fewer bench hours, and responsibilities such as people‑management, budgeting, and strategic planning.

The author notes that the boundary between the two tracks is blurry, leading to resentment (“stuck in the lab” vs. “paper‑pusher”).
