## 1. SUMMARY

This article describes the enormous challenge of chemical inventory management in medicinal chemistry departments. The author explains that drug synthesis requires a vast number of starting materials, reagents, and solvents - typically dozens just for a single multi-step synthesis. The core problem is managing this complex inventory system. The article emphasizes that poor inventory management has real costs - chemists waste time searching for materials or simply avoid certain reactions when they can't find the needed reagents. The solution requires significant investment in infrastructure and personnel, but this is necessary to maintain research productivity in drug discovery.
