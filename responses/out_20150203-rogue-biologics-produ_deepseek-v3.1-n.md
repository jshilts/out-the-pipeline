# Rogue Biologics Production (And Bonus Ignorance) (February 2015)

## 1. SUMMARY
This 2015 blog post from Science Magazine's "Pipeline" column discusses the discovery by the UK's MHRA (Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency) of an unlicensed biologics manufacturing operation in a Cambridgeshire warehouse. The facility was producing gcMAF (Gc protein-derived macrophage activating factor), an unapproved blood factor being marketed as a cancer and HIV cure. Investigators found the operation used blood plasma labeled "Not to be administered to humans," failed to meet Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) standards, and raised sterility concerns. Over 10,000 vials were seized. The author compares this to the contemporaneous boom in unregulated HCG (human chorionic gonadotropin) sales in the US, promoted as a weight-loss aid despite lacking evidence of efficacy. The article connects these examples to broader public misunderstanding of science, particularly how terms like "homeopathic" and "herbal" are misconstrued as meaning "natural and safe" rather than understanding what homeopathy actually claims to be. The author argues this scientific illiteracy enables fraudulent marketing of unproven treatments.
