- **Pterostilbene**: Large‑scale human data remain scarce. Small studies suggest modest antioxidant activity but no reproducible clinical benefit.  
- **Competing products**: Other companies (e.g., **Tru Niagen**/ChromaDex, **Nicotinamide Riboside Inc.**) entered the market with similar NR‑based supplements, often at lower price points.

**Policy impact**
