## 1. SUMMARY

This 2017 article discusses a Merck process chemistry paper that systematically investigated "salting-out" extraction techniques. Salting-out is a method where salts are added to aqueous layers during liquid-liquid extraction to improve separation of compounds, particularly those with low logP values that tend to partition into water.
