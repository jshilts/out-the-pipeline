Many countries mandated or strongly recommended mRNA vaccines for various populations, leading to significant public health benefits but also political polarization.

**Adverse Events**: Monitoring revealed rare but serious side effects including myocarditis (primarily in young males) and anaphylaxis, though the benefits continued to substantially outweigh risks for most populations.
