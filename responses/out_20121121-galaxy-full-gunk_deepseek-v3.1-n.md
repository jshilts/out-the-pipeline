Subsequent research has continued to build on these findings. Meanwhile, continued study of carbonaceous chondrites and analysis of data from missions like Cassini (which studied Saturn's moon Titan) and upcoming missions to Europa and Enceladus have reinforced the understanding that organic chemistry is widespread in our solar system.
