# How’s All That Cost‑Cutting Working Out? (June 2011)

**Policy and pricing pressure** – Parallel to cost‑cutting, governments (U.S., EU, Japan) introduced **price‑control measures** and **value‑based reimbursement** frameworks, squeezing margins on legacy products.
