A New Med-Chem Reference
30 May 2014

Over the past few years, several readers here have recommended Silverman's medicinal chemistry book as an excellent introduction and reference. I wanted to mention that there's now a third edition: The Organic Chemistry of Drug Design and Drug Action (http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0123820308/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0123820308&linkCode=as2&tag=lagniappe-20&linkId=64WZJO2ASJVDL4BL) , by Silverman and Holladay, which has just come out. Well worth a look for that part of the bookshelf.
